A round little soul who hoards seeds in surprising places.
Soft grey, with eyes like polished beads.
Pale-coated and twitchy in the best way.
Loves the sound of paper crinkling.
Has a nick in one ear from a curious encounter.
Sneezes whenever a sunbeam lands on the windowsill.
Chooses one specific corner and defends it forever.
Hides crumbs under the bedding and forgets where.
Always looks like they just heard a question.
Naps with both paws curled tight under the chin.
Brown and white in patches that look hand-painted.
Has a long tail they use as a fifth limb.
Tiny, dust-coloured, easy to overlook and very proud of it.
Squeaks at exactly nothing, twice, then settles.
Climbs anything they're told not to.
Folds their ears flat when they're concentrating.
Will eat a single oat for ten minutes.
Curls up inside a teacup like it was made for them.
Has the small confident bearing of an emperor.
Treats every leaf like an excellent gift.
